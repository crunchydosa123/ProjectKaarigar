सिद्धार्थ

Craft: मटके का व्यापार (Pottery)

Siddharth is a potter carrying on his family's pottery business. He primarily uses clay and faces challenges in sourcing good quality materials and expanding his market beyond his village. He needs information on the preferences of younger generations so he can adapt his craft and reach a wider audience.

Brief bio
सिद्धार्थ एक व्यापारी है और मटके का व्यापार उसका पारिवारिक व्यवसाय है। उसके दादाजी ने यह काम शुरू किया, फिर उसके पिताजी ने और अब वह अपने पिताजी से सीखकर इसे चला रहा है। (Siddharth is a businessman and pottery is his family business. His grandfather started this work, then his father and now he is running it after learning from his father.)
Materials & Techniques
मिट्टी (Clay). He uses specific clay and the quality of the clay is essential for making good pots.
Main challenges
अच्छी मिट्टी प्राप्त करना और अपने उत्पादों को शहर के लोगों तक पहुंचाना (Obtaining good quality clay and reaching city dwellers with his products). Selling only in his village.
Aspirations / Needs
वह युवा पीढ़ी की पसंद के अनुसार मिट्टी की चीजें बनाना चाहता है ताकि वह अधिक युवाओं तक पहुंच सके। (He wants to make clay items according to the preferences of the younger generation so that he can reach more young people.)
Suggested support
युवा पीढ़ी की पसंद के बारे में जानकारी चाहिए (Needs information about the preferences of the younger generation).
